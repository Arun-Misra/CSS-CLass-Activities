Pancakes

Ingredients

  • cup flour
  • tbsp sugar (optional, for sweetness)
  • tsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter or oil

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet mix into the dry mix and stir gently (dont overmix, a few lumps are okay).
  4. Heat a pan on medium heat and lightly grease it.
  5. Pour about Â¼ cup batter for each pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook the other side until golden.
  7. Serve warm with butter, syrup, honey, or fruit.

Enjoy you homemade pancakes!
